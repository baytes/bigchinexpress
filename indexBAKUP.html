Place An Order

Simply type out your order; listing each item or combo individually. Check to see if you have your order request correct. Submit your order along with your name & phone number. We will do an immediate callback to confirm your request. Once order is confirmed & the order total has been verified & provided to you, only then will you submit your payment. The total price will include our delivery fee already. It's as easy as that. Wait time will depend on volume, however you will be informed of ETA during your order confirmation.
