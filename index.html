Place An Order

Simply type out your order; listing each item or combo individually. Make sure you have your order request correct. Submit your order along with your name, phone number & email address. We will do an immediate callback to confirm your request. Once your order is confirmed & the order total has been verified with you, only then will you need to submit your payment. The total price will include our delivery fee already. It's as easy as that. Your wait time will depend on volume, however you will be informed of ETA during your order confirmation.



Our pricing is as follows:
*$10.00 per order for orders up to $40.00.
*$15.00 per order for orders up to $100.00.
*$100.00 and up will be handled on a case by case basis.
We except cash, PayPal,
Visa, MasterCard & American Express.
Orders will be paid for by customer upon arrival.
